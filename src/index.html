wireframing

wireframing

Wireframing, in the context of user experience design, is the act of creating user interface wireframes.


The first problem that wireframing can answer is “what are some ways our product can help our customers accomplish their goals?”


The second problem that wireframing addresses is “how do I know this solution will work?”

sketching

sketching

Next level of design is already here.We use Sketch and are very proud about it.


With sketch you don’t have to think about screen densities anymore. Just create an art board in the size of the view and Sketch does the rest.


With Sketch the designer and the developer don’t need to guess any margins and paddings between text fields anymore. Just copy and paste the margins and give it to the developer.

With Sketch every property is just easy to create and assign. No need to assign effects on layers.

fabulous

prototyping

prototyping

When we’re designing for screens, whether it’s mobile or web interfaces, it might be useful to see your work in action. With Sketch’s Prototyping features it’s easy to create interactive workflows and preview our designs as your users will see them.


Once we have created a prototype we can preview it directly in Sketch, test it on iOS Sketch Mirror, and share it with clients or colleagues by uploading to Sketch Cloud.
